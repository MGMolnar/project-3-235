Process

I started with creating the game treatment and went with a game that seems possible to do in a short amount of time so I wanted to do Snake but with a twist. This twist involves more movement as you control a second character at times and actually have an enemy rather than only running into yourself.

On the website, I started with the navigation bar then worked my way through the proposal page, applying as much information as possible. Soon after I made my home page act like a hook in a funny way to attract people to play. Then I worked on my documentation last as I'm writing this now.

It also took me 2 hours to realize I could anchor a whole div, thinking it would be invalid. Nope. That's how I got the navigation bar to look and act the way it is now.
